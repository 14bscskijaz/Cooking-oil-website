Eco-Friendly Waste Processing

We are an innovative company. An Shamali Oil specializes in collecting and converting used cooking oils and fats into vital raw materials for biofuels. Through our efforts, we significantly contribute to responsible resource management and environmental sustainability.

Utilization of Collected Oil

The oil collected by Shamali Oil undergoes treatment and purification at our facility. It's transformed into purified oil, organic impurities, and water. The purified oil is used to produce biofuels like HVO and SAF. Frying residues are turned into biogas, and other waste streams, such as plastics, are thoroughly washed, sorted, and recycled. We uphold a zero-landfill policy, ensuring minimal environmental impact and maximal resource efficiency.

Our Commitment

In support of the European Green Deal, Shamali Oil is dedicated to sustainable management and the extensive reuse of vegetable fats and oils. By actively reducing carbon emissions through responsible resource management, we contribute significantly to Europe's journey towards a carbon-neutral future by 2050. Our efforts have been recognized by the EU with the ISCC certificate, affirming our commitment to shaping a more sustainable world.
